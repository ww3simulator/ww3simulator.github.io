WW3 Simulator

Includes virtual Earth based on Eric Buneton's "Improved Atmospheric Scattering", and custom OpenStreetMap renderer; coming soon for PC & Xbox

Core Features

  • Real-time feed of current politics and wars: base your decisions on current events.
  • 50 campaigns + GPT generated ones, such as: "Simulating a Kalibur-cruise missile attack on London" and "Modern Pearl Harbor".
  • Sandbox mode: create your own missions without constraints or objectives
  • Missile defense systems such as: Israel's "Iron Dome" and America's "Patriot" system.
  • Accurate, satisfying nuclear explosions: watch ICBMs take off, travel across the world, and strike their target.
  • Mix and match eras: there is a database of military technology, with over 500 different units that are unlockable. Mix and match eras from WW1, WW2, and modern warfare.

Vision

The vision of WW3 Simulator was inspired by war sims like Command: Modern Operations and DEFCON / ICBM. We wanted to take the core of those games, and make it 3D instead of 2D.
